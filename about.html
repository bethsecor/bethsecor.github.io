About Me

Beth Secor is currently a student at the Turing School of Software and Design, studying full stack web development. She has a M.A. in Biostatistics from the University of California, Berkeley, and a B.S. in Mathematics from Regis University. Before starting at Turing, she worked as a statistician for Brigham and Women's Hospital in Boston and the Colorado Department of Public Health and Environment in Denver.

In her spare time, she enjoys painting and drawing, reading anything from science fiction to Russian literature, and dog sledding at Winter Park.
